Frequently Asked Questions

How does the book exchange work?

Our exchange platform allows you to trade gently-used books with other members. List your books, receive exchange credits, and use those credits to request books from other users. Each credit equals one book exchange.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For physical store purchases, we also accept cash and contactless payments.

Can I return a purchased book?

Yes! Unread books in original condition can be returned within 30 days of purchase. Please keep your receipt for hassle-free returns.

How do I participate in author events?

Check our Events page for upcoming author meetups. Most events are free for members, but some may require registration due to limited seating.

What condition should exchange books be in?

Books should be in good condition with no missing pages or significant damage. We reserve the right to reject books that don't meet our quality standards.
